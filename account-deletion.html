Last updated: [insert date]

1. Overview

This Account & Data Deletion Policy explains how you can delete your account and personal data in the Expense Tracker – Smart Budget Manager app.

2. Delete Your Account from Inside the App

You can permanently delete your account directly from within the app:

  • Open the Expense Tracker – Smart Budget Manager app.
  • Go to the Profile tab.
  • Tap the "Delete Account" button.
  • Confirm the deletion in the pop-up dialog.

Once confirmed, our system will:

  • Delete your user account from Firebase Authentication.
  • Delete your transaction data, categories, income and expense entries linked to your UID.
  • Delete any app profile data stored in Firestore related to this account.

Important: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

3. Request Deletion by Email

If you are unable to delete your account from inside the app, you may request deletion by email.

Please contact us at:

Email: [your support email]

Include the following details:

  • The email address used to sign in to the app.
  • Your UID (if available – visible on the Profile screen).

We will process your request and delete your account and associated data within 30 days.

4. What Data Is Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is removed:

  • Your Firebase user account.
  • Your transaction history (income, expenses, categories, notes).
  • Your app profile data and Pro status stored in Firestore.

5. What Data May Be Retained

Some data may be retained where necessary:

  • Google Play purchase history is managed by Google and cannot be deleted by us. You can review this in your Google account.
  • Limited backup or security logs may be temporarily stored for fraud prevention and legal compliance. These are not used for advertising and are deleted after a reasonable retention period.

6. Subscriptions & Purchases

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel any active purchase or subscription made through Google Play.

To cancel a subscription:

  • Open the Google Play Store app.
  • Go to Payments & subscriptions.
  • Select the relevant subscription and tap Cancel.
